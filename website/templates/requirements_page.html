Requirements

The following are the requirements to become a Community Advisor (CA) at Colby College. These requirements help ensure that CAs are prepared to create a supportive, inclusive, and engaging residential environment for all students.

Candidates must be enrolled as full-time students at Colby College for the 2024-2025 academic year and live on campus.

Applicants must have a cumulative and semester GPA of at least 2.5 to qualify and maintain eligibility.

Candidates must be in good student conduct standing, with no current involvement in any disciplinary or legal proceedings.

Attendance at all training sessions is required, including Fall, JanPlan, and Spring sessions. This includes being present from the first day of training until the last scheduled day, with few exceptions.

CAs must demonstrate a commitment to fostering an inclusive environment that respects diversity and promotes understanding among all residents.

Candidates should possess or be willing to develop strong leadership, communication, and conflict-resolution skills to effectively support and guide their peers.

Please complete all requirements to continue to the application.
