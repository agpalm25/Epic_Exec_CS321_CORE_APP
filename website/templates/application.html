Substance-Free housing at Colby College is dedicated to providing a housing experience in which there is zero substance use of any kind within the vicinity of the residential community. Further defined, this includes that residents of the building will not consume alcohol, smoke or use tobacco products of any kind (including use of vape pens, etc.), or smoke or use any marijuana or any marijuana-related product of any kind while inside any space of the designated residential building. Additionally, Colby College is a smoke free campus, meaning that smoking of any kind is prohibited on Colby property [smoking pertains to all forms of smoking and is not limited to tobacco products].
